I'm passionate about design and it's potential to change the world.

I’ve taken two courses on Drafting and Design, and have been positively enthralled. I love imagining a design to make people’s lives better and bringing it to life through CAD. I’ve designed furniture, bike wheels, kitchens, and urban settings such as a parklet as part of my second year of Drafting. While I’m relatively new with two years of experience with Vectorworks in school, I’m a strong student who’s shown a lot a potential using it and I’m a fast learner who loves buildings and design.
