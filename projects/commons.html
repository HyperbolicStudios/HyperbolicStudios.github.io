What do we need today?

What do people want in a library? Ultimately, everyone wants something different. Some people want a silent cave with nothing but a candle for light, whereas some people want human contact, with loud voices and music and streams of daylight coming through the windows. The challenge of a designer is to reconcile these multi-faceted, conflicting demands into one harmonious design.
