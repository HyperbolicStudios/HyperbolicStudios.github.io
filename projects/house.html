Small House

An exploration into the fundamentals of housing construction.

Building a small house.

This was a technically challenging project that involved several different constraints:
  • Size: the house must be under 1000 sqare meters.
  • Terrain: model a challenging terrain and design a house around it.
  • User constraints: imagine a user and his lifestyle.
  • Materials: the materials should be local and astetically pleasing.




Beautiful efficiency.

Inspired by both the Usonian style in the Eastern US and local BC construction styles and techniques, the house is minimalist but cozy, featuring wood for the solid walls while using floor-to-cieling windows to provide ample natural light. Exposed wood beams highlight, not hide, the structural elements of the house.




Zen Landscaping

The landscaping of the house accentualized the minimalism of the house, while providing shade and colour to the souroundings.




Terrain

I took advantage of Vectorworks Landscape tools to model a realistic terrain for the house to be constructed on.
